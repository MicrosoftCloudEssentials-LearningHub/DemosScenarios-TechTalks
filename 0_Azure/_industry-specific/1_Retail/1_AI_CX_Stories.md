> [!NOTE]
> **Cross-cutting Pattern: Multi-Agent RAG**
> - Many of these solutions `rely on multiple specialized agents retrieving enterprise data and augmenting generation with factual grounding.`
> - This approach `enables scalable automation, accurate answers, and orchestration across domains by combining retrieval with generation and agent collaboration.`
> - Common tools `include Azure OpenAI, Copilot Studio, and Microsoft 365.`
